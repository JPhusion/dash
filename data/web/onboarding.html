Hi 👋

I'm Dash. I'll keep you company while you focus. This walkthrough takes about a minute and covers every gesture, sound, and screen I'll throw at you.

Reopen this any time from dash.local → Settings → Replay welcome tutorial.

What's your name?

My name's Dash, but I'd love to know yours. I'll use it to label the Wi-Fi network you connect to me with.

Taps on my head

Tap firmly on the top face. I count how many taps land in a row.

1 tapfriendly chirp — I noticed you
👆
2 taps faststart a focus session · double-tap again to stop
✌️
3 taps fastthree-note chime + happy face (no other side effect)
🎵

Tap timing is mouse-fast — under 300 ms between taps. I ignore impacts that are too hard (knocks, drops) or that come with rotation (flicks, pickups) so taps stay clean.

None of these change your settings or sessions. They're personality. The two motions that DO matter are covered on the next two cards.

Focus sessions

A session is a timed focus block. While one runs, my eyes go focused and I track your progress.

Startdouble-tap me, or tap "Start session" in the app
HalfwayI flash a quick happy face to encourage you
Donecelebration animation + chime when the timer hits 0
End earlydouble-tap again or use "End early" in the app

Stats live on the Stats tab — total hours, current streak, recent sessions.

The face-up menu

Set me on the desk with my screen pointing up at the ceiling. After about 2 seconds the menu opens. From here you can launch games and tweak my settings — no phone required.

Openscreen facing up for ~2 s
⬆️
Scroll listtilt me toward you / away from you
↕️
Selecttap my head
👆
Adjust a valuetilt left / right · hold to keep going
↔️
Closejust put me down — menu exits after 2 s
⬇️

Menu items: Games (Reaction · Bop It), Volume, Session min, Sleep timeout, Tap sens. Tap on Games to open the games submenu; tap on a setting to enter its bar — then tilt left/right to change it. Holding the cube tilted keeps stepping the bar ~2× per second.

Reaction: wait for "GO!" and tap as fast as you can — 5 rounds.
Bop It: I show TAP / FLICK / SHAKE — you've got a shrinking window to obey.

Sleep & wake

If you leave me alone for ~3 minutes my eyes droop and I doze off to save power.

Auto-sleepdrowsy → asleep after the configured timeout
Sleep on demandflip me so the screen faces the table — hold ~3 s
Wake mepick me up, or tap the pad

Triple-tap used to be the sleep gesture — it's now just a chime. Sleep is screen-down only so you can't trigger it by accident during a session.
