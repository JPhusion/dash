Taps on my head

Tap firmly on my top face. I sense the impulse through the IMU so anywhere on top works — you don't need to hit the cap-touch pad exactly.

1 tapfriendly chirp + blink — I noticed you
👆
2 taps faststart a focus session · double-tap again to end it
✌️
3 taps fastjust a little chime + happy face — no other effect
🎵

Tap timing is mouse-fast — under 300 ms between taps to count as a chain.
Tap filtering: I ignore impacts that are too hard (knocks, drops), too long (sustained shaking), or that come with rotation (flicks, pickups), so accidental taps stay rare.
Not for sleeping — three taps used to put me to sleep; now they don't. See the Sleep card.

Focus sessions

A session is a timed focus block. While one runs, my eyes go focused and I track your progress.

Startdouble-tap me, or tap "Start session" in the app
HalfwayI flash a quick happy face to encourage you
Donecelebration animation + chime when the timer hits 0
End earlydouble-tap again or use "End early" in the app

Stats live on the Stats tab — total hours, current streak, recent sessions.

The face-up menu

Set me on the desk with my screen pointing straight up at the ceiling. After about 2 seconds the menu opens. From here you launch games and tweak my settings — no phone needed.

Openscreen facing up · hold ~2 s
⬆️
Scroll the listtilt me toward you (down) / away from you (up)
↕️
Select / confirmtap my head
👆
Adjust a valuetilt left = down, right = up · hold to keep stepping (2 Hz)
↔️
Closejust put me down — menu auto-exits after 2 s of not face-up
⬇️

Items in order: Games → Volume → Session min → Sleep timeout → Tap sens.
• Tap on Games to open the games submenu (Reaction or Bop It).
• Tap on a setting to enter its slider, then tilt left/right to adjust. Tap again to return to the list.
• Putting me down at any depth exits the whole menu — no "Back" needed.

Reaction: wait for "GO!" and tap as fast as you can — 5 rounds, scored on reaction time.
Bop It: I show TAP / FLICK / SHAKE — you've got a shrinking window to do the right move.

Sleep & wake

When I sleep, my eyes close and the screen blanks out. You can wake me instantly with a shake.

How I go to sleep

Auto-sleepeyes droop → close after the configured idle timeout (default 3 min)
😴
Manual sleepscreen face-down on the table — hold ~3 s
⬇️

How I wake up

Shake meinstant — IMU motion interrupt
🤝
Tap the cap-touch padfallback if shake-wake isn't responding
☝️

Triple-tap doesn't sleep me — only screen-down or auto-sleep does, so you can't put me to sleep by accident during a session.
Screen-down ≠ upside-down. Screen-down means the display itself touches the table. Upside-down (cap-touch face on the table) just makes me surprised — see the reactions card.
Battery note: I trade some standby current (~0.8 mA) for the shake-to-wake feature. A 2 Ah pack lasts roughly 2–3 days of standby.
