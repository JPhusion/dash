Hi 👋

I'm Dash. I'll keep you company while you focus. This walkthrough takes about a minute and covers every gesture, sound, and screen I'll throw at you.

Reopen this any time from dash.local → Settings → Replay welcome tutorial.

What's your name?

My name's Dash, but I'd love to know yours. I'll use it to label the Wi-Fi network you connect to me with.

I show how I feel with eyes

Look at my little OLED face. I'll glance around when idle, focus when you're working, look surprised when you tap me, and close my eyes when I'm sleepy.

Taps on my head

Tap firmly on the top face — the touch pad. I count how many taps in a row.

1 tapfriendly chirp — I noticed you
👆
2 taps faststart a focus session · double-tap again to stop
✌️
3 taps fastput me to sleep
😴

A double-tap chime confirms the gesture landed. Tap timing is mouse-fast — under 300 ms between taps.

Focus sessions

A session is a timed focus block. While one runs, my eyes go focused and I track your progress.

Startdouble-tap me, or tap "Start session" in the app
HalfwayI flash a quick happy face to encourage you
Donecelebration animation + chime when the timer hits 0
End earlydouble-tap again or use "End early" in the app

Stats live on the Stats tab — total hours, current streak, recent sessions.

Quick games

Two mini-games live on me. Two ways to open them:

Long-press my touch pad OR flick me forward opens the games menu on my screen
Shake in the menu → next item
Tap in the menu → select highlighted game

Reaction: wait for "GO!" and tap as fast as you can — 5 rounds.
Bop It: I show TAP / ← / → / SHAKE — you've got a shrinking window to obey.

Sleep & wake

If you leave me alone for ~3 minutes my eyes droop and I doze off to save power.

Auto-sleepdrowsy → asleep after the configured timeout
Sleep on demandtriple-tap, or flip me face-down for 3 s
Wake metouch the pad, or pick me up
